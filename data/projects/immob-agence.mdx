**Immob Agency** was designed to enrich my experience in development, especially
in the use of modern technologies such as Next.js, Prisma, Zod, and Shadcn/ui.
This application offers a practical approach to simplify real estate management
in an intuitive way.

## Technologies

- **NextJs**
- **Prisma**
- **Tailwind CSS**
- **Shadcn/ui**
